A demonstration of how small businesses can save on their electricity bills by installing a battery, even if they don't have solar panels!
Three scenarios are modelled:
1. Energy usage and costs on a fixed tariff
2. Energy usage and costs on a Time-of-Use tariff
3. Energy usage and costs on a Time-of-Use tariff, using a battery optimised to shift load to cheap times to use energy

## Things this model doesn't include
- Revenue from participation in flexibility and capacity markets
- Disribution network charges, which incentivise shifting load to less busy times
